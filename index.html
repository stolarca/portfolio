Stefan Sagmeister and Jessica Walsh are two of the most influential figures in contemporary graphic design, known for a partnership that redefined the boundaries between commercial art, personal expression, and provocative storytelling. Their collaboration, Sagmeister & Walsh, remains a landmark in the industry for its "gutsy" approach to visual communication.
